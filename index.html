VELR: Efficient Video Reward Feedback via Ensemble Latent Reward Models

Anonymous Project Page

Below we provide side-by-side comparisons of VELR with two strong video reward models, UnifiedReward and CausalVQA.

The results are based on Wan-2.1. More videos are on the way.
